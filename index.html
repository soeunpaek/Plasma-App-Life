Title: Plasma Treatment Green Industry for citizens of Industrial city in a 4IR

Since the Industrial Revolution, various environmental pollution problems have occurred due to the development and use of resources.
Especially in industrial cities, already the environment exceeded the natural purification capacity occurring various diseases with the ecosystem being destroyed.
In this sense focus on increasing the regeneration capacity for the ecosystem restoration of industrial cities.
How can make a green treatment so that citizens could safely breathe and healthily live in an industrial city of multi-crisis (climate change, industrial-pollutions, and pandemic) in a 4IR era?
The Smart Plasma Plants RE100 (SPPR) combines InfoTech and Biotech based on renewable energy.
SPPR supports climate systems as they systemically transform themselves to improve diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) in science, technology, engineering, and medicine (STEM).
The AI auto-sensor system produces optimally treated species that transforms atmospheric (real) substance as data.
The (Cold) Plasma plays a role in anti-infection the treatment solution to industrial pollutants compounds with the variant’s viruses.
Plants by photosynthesis (chemical process) consume CO2 and produce O2 that humans need to breathe a sustainable circular system process for the essential vital source for the life.
All processes operate by the 100% renewable energy system that adapts to Net-Zero action as an alternative energy system for the mitigation of CO2 by fossil fuels that protects against global warming.
The enterprise and government for desirable industry must respond to industrial crisis (the treatment of pollution) for safe & healthy citizens (humans) in industrial cities (environment).
The industrial city's citizens have the right to have clean environmental rights.
SPPR is a win-win industrial city project for the citizens of the industrial city.
